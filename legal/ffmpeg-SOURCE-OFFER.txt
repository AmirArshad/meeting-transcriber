FFmpeg — GPLv3 source offer (AvaNevis installers)

AvaNevis installers bundle ffmpeg 8.0.1 as a separate subprocess (Opus
compression). Prebuilt binaries come from third-party hosts:

  Windows: gyan.dev FFmpeg 8.0.1 essentials (GPLv3)
  macOS:   shaka-project/static-ffmpeg-binaries ffmpeg n8.0.1 arm64 (GPLv3)

Pinned URLs and SHA-256 checksums: legal/FFMPEG-COMPLIANCE.json

Corresponding upstream source for FFmpeg 8.0.1
--------------------------------------------

Official release tarball (verified SHA-256 in FFMPEG-COMPLIANCE.json):

  https://ffmpeg.org/releases/ffmpeg-8.0.1.tar.xz

GitHub Releases for AvaNevis attach the same file as ffmpeg-8.0.1.tar.xz next
to Windows/macOS installers. You can also open Settings → About → Open
third-party notices in the app for bundled attribution files.

Windows prepare-build may copy upstream license text from the gyan.dev archive
as legal/ffmpeg-upstream-* inside the installer resources.
